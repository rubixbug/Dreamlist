Goofy

Quests

  • Fishing Expedition:
    1. Find Goofy's fishing rod by the pond near his house.
    2. Ask Goofy "How do I fish?"
    3. Catch a fish!
    4. Show Goofy what you caught.
    5. Follow Goofy to his stall and talk to him.
    6. Use the sign next to the stall to fix it (for free)
    7. Speak to Goofy one more time.
    8. QUEST COMPLETE!
  • A Warm Welcome:
    1. Speak to Goofy.
    2. Find 3 Basil in the Meadow and 3 Oregano in the Plaza.
    3. Speak to Goofy again.
    4. Cook any meal using Basil or Oregano, then speak to Goofy one more time!
    5. QUEST COMPLETE!
  • Photographic Memory:
    1. Speak to Goofy, he'll ask you to take photos of things around the village.
    2. Take photos of:
      • An apple tree (Plaza)
      • A raspberry bush (Plaza/Meadow)
      • A banana tree (Meadow/Beach)
    3. Return to Goofy who will ask you for more photos.
      • A rabbit (Meadow)
      • A squirrel (Plaza)
    4. Return to Goofy again who will now want photos of flowers.
      • Red daisy (Meadow)
      • Green rising penstemon (Meadow)
      • White and pink falling penstemon (Plaza)
      • Dandelion (Plaza)
    5. Return to Goofy a final time.
    6. QUEST COMPLETE!
  • The Mysterious Wreck:
    1. Go to Dazzle Beach. Find the island across the water from Goofy's stall. On the far side of that island is a paddle in the sand. Pick it up.
    2. Speak to Goofy and harvest the following materials:
      • 30 Hardwood (Forgotten Lands, Frosted Heights, Sunlit Plateu, Glade of Trust, Forest of Valor)
      • 7 Rope (8 Fiber each)
      • 25 Clay (Glade of Trust, Sunlit Plateu, Forgotten Lands)
      • 10 Iron Ingots (5 Iron Ore & 1 Coal)
    3. Return to Goofy.
    4. Craft a Raft Repair Kit and speak to Goofy again.
    5. Take Goofy to the raft on Dazzle Beach & interact with it to use Repair Kit.
    6. Speak to Goofy a final time.
    7. QUEST COMPLETE!
  • Dinner With a Friend:
    1. Speak to Goofy, he'll ask for help preparing dinner for Mickey coming over.
    2. Collect the following seafood items:
      • 2 Shrimp (Dazzle Beach)
      • 2 Clams (Dazzle Beach)
      • 2 Scallops (Dazzle Beach)
    3. Return to Goofy and he will give you the recipe for Bouillabaisse. Read the recipe in your inventory.
    4. Collect two tomatoes and two other random vegetables.
    5. Cook two Bouillabaisses and give them to Goofy.
    6. QUEST COMPLETE!
  • Photo Fervor:
    1. Speak to Goofy, he'll ask you to take photos of the following things:
      • The Swamps Giant Willow Tree (Glade of Trust)
      • The Plateu's Elephant Graveyard (Sunlit Plateu)
      • Scrooge's Store (Plaza)
      • Remy's Restaurant (Plaza)
      • Dream Castle (Plaza)
    2. Speak to Goofy again and he will ask for photos of the Pillars around the valley.
      • Pillar of Friendshuip (Peaceful Meadow)
      • Pillar of Power (Dazzle Beach)
      • Pillar of Courage (Forest of Valor)
      • Pillar of Trust (Glade of Trust)
    3. Speak to Goofy again and he'll ask for a selfie.
    4. Equip a silly hat and take a selfie with Goofy.
    5. QUEST COMPLETE!
  • Scrapbook Blitz:
    1. At Frienship Level 10, speak to Goofy who will ask for pictures of the villagers.
    2. Take a photo of Mickey, and then Donald, and then 4 other villagers that you'd like.
    3. Speak to Goofy again to receive the Scrapbook. Place the Scrapbook somewhere in the village.
    4. Take one final photo with the gathered villagers.
    5. Speak to Goofy a final time.
    6. QUEST COMPLETE!
